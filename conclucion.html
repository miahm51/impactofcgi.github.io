Conclusion

In conclusion, CGI has fundamentally transformed our experience of visual media. It has reshaped our perception of realism, unlocked new creative possibilities, and provided artists with greater freedom in film, gaming, and anime. By blending the digital and physical realms, CGI has elevated the standards for visual quality and immersive storytelling. Its evolution from basic graphics to intricate, lifelike imagery has not only shifted audience expectations but also empowered creators to bring their most imaginative concepts to life, enriching the media landscape with a wealth of limitless narratives.
